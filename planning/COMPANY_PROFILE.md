### Team Charter values (internal — see TEAM_CHARTER.md)
Authenticity · Accountability · Adaptability · Collaboration · Discipline

### Agile Operating Model
Triple A explicitly positions as an **Agile construction firm** — not traditional command-and-control.

**Corporate vision (Agile edition):**
> "To be the most adaptive and client-centric construction firm in the industry, transforming the built environment through iterative excellence and sustainable innovation."

**Corporate mission (Agile edition):**
> Deliver high-quality real estate and construction by integrating Agile methodologies — collaborative, human-centric, functional value at every build stage.

**Core mandate:**
> De-risk construction for clients. Move away from the "rigid blueprint" trap via constant feedback loop — final building reflects what is needed today, not just what was planned years ago.

### Six Operating Principles
1. **Value flow** — deliver what the business needs, not just initial plan
2. **Client engagement** — frequent interactions, shared ownership
3. **Adaptive planning** — iterations, anticipation, adaptation over rigid planning
4. **Individual innovation** — team members as source of value
5. **Group accountability** — shared responsibility, no silos
6. **Context-specific strategies** — tailor approach per project

### Policy Pledges
- Safety & workforce/environment well-being
- Every phase delivers MVP or measurable value
- Open-book communication — no hidden costs or delays

### The 12 Agile Principles (Construction-adapted)
1. Customer satisfaction via early/continuous milestone delivery
2. Welcome change for client competitive advantage
3. Frequent delivery — milestones: **Foundation → Shell → Finishing**
4. Daily sync: Architects, Engineers, Builders, Clients
5. Motivated tradespeople with environment and support
6. Face-to-face conversation most efficient on-site
7. **Functional progress** = work completed (primary metric)
8. Sustainable pace — avoid burnout and errors
9. Technical excellence and structural integrity
10. Simplicity — maximize work NOT done (reduce waste)
11. Self-organizing site managers make real-time decisions
12. Retrospectives after every project phase

### Agile Foundation (Manifesto adaptation)
| Over | Under |
|---|---|
| Individuals & interactions | Processes & tools |
| Working structures | Comprehensive documentation |
| Client collaboration | Contract negotiation |
| Responding to change | Following a plan |
