### Document 3 — Abraham's Document: Nigerian National Quality Policy (NNQP)
**Source:** [Adobe Acrobat](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:EU:c75e0cc0-e897-4f3c-8220-f5c6b443e8a0)  
**Full PDF (public):** https://downloads.unido.org/ot/60/14/6014367/NNQP.pdf

**Title:** Nigeria National Quality Policy (NNQP) 2020–2025  
**Subtitle:** National Commitment to Quality of Products, Services & Persons  
**Motto:** For a more competitive and diversified economy

#### Purpose
Strengthen and develop the **National Quality Infrastructure (NQI)** to achieve:
- Accelerated economic growth
- Increased exports
- Safe, quality products at competitive prices
- Environmental protection
- Poverty reduction and economic prosperity

#### Primary Objective
Ensure goods and services emanating from, imported into, and traded in Nigeria meet purchaser/consumer expectations and regulatory requirements in local and export markets.

#### National Quality Infrastructure (NQI) Pillars
1. **Standards** — Harmonization via Standards Organization of Nigeria (SON) as National Standards Body
2. **Metrology** — Scientific, industrial, and legal measurement for trade certainty
3. **Accreditation** — NINAS (when established) accredits conformity assessment bodies
4. **Conformity Assessment** — Inspection, testing, product/system certification

#### Relevance to Real Estate App
- **Quality Checks** on site daily reports should align with SON standards and National Building Code
- **Material tracking** (cement, steel, etc.) should reference certified suppliers and test results
- **Engineer/contractor profiles** should track certifications and accreditation status
- **Document Management** should store conformity certificates, test reports, building approvals
- **Property Development** estates should track compliance with technical regulations
- Complements Nigeria National Building Code (2007): administration, technical processes, enforcement, health & safety

## Part C: Analysis — What This Actually Is

Samuel's list reads "random and weird" because it mixes **four different products** into one vision:

| Layer | What it is | Examples from the list |
|---|---|---|
| **Consumer marketplace** | Zillow-style public site | Search, compare, AI match, tours, maps |
| **Agency CRM/ERP** | Back-office for a realtor | CRM, agents, commissions, marketing |
| **Developer/construction OS** | Build & sell estates | BOQ, site logs, plot maps, engineering |
| **Full company ERP** | Run the whole business | HR, payroll, procurement, vehicles, petty cash |

The Adobe documents ground this in **Triple A Realty Projects Ltd.** — a Nigerian developer/project manager that:
- Markets properties in **Abuja** (Guzape, Katampe, Dawaki, Lugbe, Jikwoyi, Mpape)
- Runs **construction sites** with daily reporting
- Cares about **quality standards** (NNQP reference)
- Needs **social media ops** tied to property inventory

### Reporting Cadence (must be automated)

| Frequency | What | Who |
|---|---|---|
| Daily | Site logs, morning/close briefings | Foreman → PM |
| Weekly | Project status report | PM → Management + Client |
| Monthly | Inventory reconciliation | Store Manager → Management |
| Monthly | Financial progress report | PM → Management |
| Same day | HSE, security, ethics incidents | Anyone → PM + HR |

### Critical Business Rules for the App

1. **No construction without FCDA planning approval and building permit** — project gate in software
2. **Material requests require Foreman or PM authorisation** — approval workflow
3. **Daily site logs due before foreman leaves site** — mobile form with submission deadline
4. **Design discrepancies reported within 24 hours** — SLA-tracked tasks
5. **Client sign-off on major design variations** — change order + e-signature
6. **Zero unrecorded material issuances** — inventory audit trail
7. **Charter acknowledgement on onboarding** — HR e-signature in personnel file
8. **Subcontractor compliance:** NSITF, tax clearance, professional memberships on file
9. **Social media:** site photos require management approval before posting
10. **Whistleblower channel** for fraud/corruption — confidential reporting module

### Disciplinary Framework (HR module)

Minor → Moderate → Serious → Gross Misconduct (summary dismissal for theft, bribery, violence, fraud).

Triggers include: record falsification, safety breaches, harassment, substance abuse on site.

### Community & Corporate Commitments
- Local sourcing and Abuja/FCT employment preference
- Community engagement (noise, dust, traffic, access)
- FIRS / FCT-IRS tax compliance; ICPC/EFCC anti-corruption stance
